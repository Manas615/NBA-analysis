# NBA Salary Cap Rules (2024-25 Season)

## Salary Cap
- The 2024-25 NBA salary cap is $140,588,000
- The luxury tax threshold is $170,814,000
- The salary cap applies to total team payroll

## Trade Salary Matching Rules

### Over-the-Cap Teams (125% Rule)
- Teams over the salary cap can only acquire a player via trade if the incoming salary does not exceed 125% of the outgoing salary plus $100,000
- Formula: Max Incoming = (Outgoing Salary × 1.25) + $100,000
- This applies to EACH side of the trade independently

### Under-the-Cap Teams
- Teams under the salary cap can absorb salary up to the amount of cap space available
- They are not bound by the 125% matching rule
- They can take on more salary than they send out, up to their remaining cap space

### Tax Apron Rules
- First Apron: $178,132,000 — limits sign-and-trade and trade exceptions
- Second Apron: $188,931,000 — additional restrictions on roster construction

## Player Salary Ranges
- Minimum salary: ~$1,119,563 (0 years experience)
- Maximum salary: ~$51,415,938 (10+ years, 35% of cap)
- Supermax: ~$60,327,150 (designated veteran extension)

## Exceptions
- Mid-Level Exception (MLE): ~$12,858,000
- Bi-Annual Exception: ~$4,682,000
- Minimum salary exception: Always available
- Trade exceptions: Valid for 1 year after trade

## Restricted Free Agency
- Teams can match any offer sheet to their restricted free agent
- Qualifying offer must be tendered by June 29
- RFA has the right to accept the qualifying offer (1-year deal)

## Bird Rights
- Larry Bird rights: 3+ consecutive seasons with same team — max salary
- Early Bird rights: 2+ consecutive seasons — up to 175% of previous salary
- Non-Bird rights: Less than 2 seasons — up to 120% of minimum salary
